What this tool does

This portal organizes faculty research strengths across multiple communities and uses AI-generated matches to suggest competitive collaborative teams aligned with federal opportunities. Department and program sites are scoped to their own rosters, while the Institute-wide site supports cross-group team building.
  • Maps scientific strengths across basic, translational, and population science.
  • Provides curated two-investigator and multi-investigator team suggestions.
  • Routes keyword-based exploration through unified faculty profiles.

Faculty profiles

A consolidated view of research areas, model systems, experimental capabilities, and publications (where available) across communities.
  • Review lab representation and scoped membership.
  • Identify natural collaborators using shared themes and approaches.
  • Use term filters to quickly find experts across domains.
